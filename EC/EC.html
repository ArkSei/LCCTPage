1.LCCT provides learning opportunities to Early Childhood pupils in a supportive learning environment that will enrich pupils’ fundamental learning and logical and critical thinking skills for creatives and independent academic undertakings.

2.LCCT creates a socially stimulating learning environment in the community, where teamwork and leadership values of the pupils will be synergistically developed through collaboration among pupils, parents, and teachers.

3.LCCT encourages pupils to grow in their Catholic Faith and Gospel Values through genuine and active participation in exposure, outreach programs, religious, and other spiritual activities.

4.LCCT nurtures pupils’ emotional development to help them reach their potentials as grade school pupils through a caring, safe, and family-like environment.

5.LCCT promotes physical awareness of one’s capabilities, self-confidence, self-esteem, and appreciation of others’ talents and skills through extra and co-curricular activities.

6.LCCT advocates St. Augustine’s ideals of Community of Friends and Teachings for a holistic approach in delivering Augustinian-Catholic Education.
